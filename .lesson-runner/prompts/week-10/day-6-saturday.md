# Lesson 6 (Module 10) — Interview & Quiz

## What You Covered This Week
This week you learned Next.js fundamentals and ported the embroidery store to the App Router:
- **Lesson 1:** Next.js App Router architecture, file-based routing with `page.tsx` and `layout.tsx`, route groups with `(parentheses)` — built the store skeleton with storefront, account, and checkout layouts
- **Lesson 2:** Server Components vs Client Components mental model, `"use client"` directive, serialization boundary, "push the boundary down" principle — built server-rendered product pages with client-side cart interactions
- **Lesson 3:** Loading UI with `loading.tsx` and skeleton patterns, error handling with `error.tsx` and `reset()`, `not-found.tsx` and `notFound()` — built store loading skeletons, product 404, error fallbacks
- **Lesson 4:** `<Link>` component for navigation, `useRouter` for programmatic navigation, `usePathname`, dynamic routes `[slug]`, catch-all routes, `generateStaticParams`, breadcrumbs — built product detail pages with full navigation
- **Lesson 5:** Built the complete embroidery store in Next.js — full routing, three layout contexts, server/client split, dynamic product pages, loading/error states, category navigation
